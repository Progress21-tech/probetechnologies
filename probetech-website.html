Probe Technologies Limited (Probetech) was built on a simple belief: the best technology doesn't just work — it transforms lives. We are a Nigerian-born, globally ambitious software development company at the forefront of innovation.

Born From Real Problems

Probetech didn't begin in a boardroom. It began with the frustrations of everyday Nigerian life — a patient turned away from a hospital because their records were missing, a university student losing their rent to a fraudulent agent, a business shutting down because the national grid collapsed again.

We saw these problems not as misfortunes to endure, but as challenges to engineer solutions for. So we did. We assembled a team of innovators, engineers, and designers who believe that Africa's problems are Africa's greatest opportunity — and that technology is the bridge.

Today, Probetech operates at the intersection of technology, design, and social impact — building products and delivering services that make a measurable difference in the lives of millions.

Mediconnect — Nigeria's
Unified Medical Record System
Imagine a NIN for healthcare. Every Nigerian gets a unique Health Identification Number (HIN) that stores their complete medical history — diagnoses, prescriptions, allergies, lab results — on a secure national cloud. Any authorized doctor, at any hospital, can access it instantly.
  • Unique Health ID per Nigerian — like NIN, but for medical records
  • Secure cloud storage accessible to authorized healthcare providers nationwide
  • AI agents that handle admin tasks so doctors focus on clinical care
  • Automated scheduling, clinical notes, billing & patient follow-ups
  • Emergency access via QR code for first responders
  • Aggregated, anonymized data for public health insights

AccommoLink — Verified Student Accommodation
As UNILAG students, we've seen the accommodation crisis firsthand — students scammed out of hundreds of thousands of Naira by fake agents. AccommoLink is the solution: a trusted marketplace where every agent is verified, every payment is protected by escrow, and every student is safe.
  • Agent verification via BVN, NIN and CAC registration — no fake agents
  • Escrow payment system — funds released only after physical confirmation
  • Student reviews and agent ratings for full transparency
  • 360° virtual property tours — inspect before you travel
  • Digital tenancy agreements stored securely on the platform
  • Built-in dispute resolution and mediation system

SolarConnect — Nigeria's Renewable Energy Marketplace
Nigeria's grid collapses almost daily. Generators cost businesses millions. SolarConnect is the marketplace that connects the entire solar supply chain — manufacturers, investors, and distributors — in one trusted ecosystem, accelerating Nigeria's transition to clean, reliable energy.
  • Manufacturers list and sell solar products to a nationwide distributor network
  • Investors discover and fund vetted renewable energy projects with full transparency
  • Distributors access inventory, trade financing, and order management tools
  • End consumers buy verified solar systems with installation support
  • Real-time project tracking and transparent ROI reporting for investors
  • Covers panels, inverters, batteries and full solar installation packages
